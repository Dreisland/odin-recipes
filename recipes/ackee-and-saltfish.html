Ackee & Saltfish

Ingredients

  • 1 lb salted fish
  • 1 can of ackee
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 large fresh chopped tomato
  • half each of chopped red, green and yellow peppers
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 sprigs of thyme
  • half a scotch bonnet pepper, deseeded and finely choppedsalt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Soak the saltfish overnight in a bowl of water. After soaking throw the water away. Add fresh water to a pan and boil the saltfish for 20 mins. Drain, de-bone and peel any skin that may remain on the fish.
  2. Put the oil in the pan and add the onion, garlic, peppers, tomato and thyme. Fry on a very low heat for 15 mins, cover the pan if necessary.
